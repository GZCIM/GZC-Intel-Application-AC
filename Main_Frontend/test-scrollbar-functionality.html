Manual Test Instructions
To manually test the scrollbar functionality:
  • 1. Open the main application and navigate to the Portfolio component
  • 2. Ensure there are enough portfolio positions to trigger scrolling
  • 3. Look for a green scrollbar on the right edge (vertical scrolling)
  • 4. Look for a green scrollbar on the bottom edge (horizontal scrolling)
  • 5. Click and drag the scrollbar thumbs to scroll the content
  • 6. Verify that the content scrolls smoothly and the thumbs move correctly
  • 7. Check browser console for debug logs about scrollbar state
